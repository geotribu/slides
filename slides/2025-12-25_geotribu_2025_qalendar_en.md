## Geotribu :tm:

- not an association neither a structure
- technically a domain name & a GitHub organization
- basically a spontaneous grouping of geoenthusiasts

### Core values

- blogging and watching FOSS4G, in [fr](https://geotribu.fr/) and [en](https://blog.geotribu.net/)
- sharing what we know
- have some fun, sometimes trolling :smirk:

Contribution on [GitHub](https://github.com/geotribu) & Matrix ([fr](https://matrix.to/#/#geotribu-agora:matrix.org) + [en](https://matrix.to/#/#geotribu-agora-en:matrix.org)) open and welcome !

### :calendar: Day 22

Marseille - Paris connections...

![Qalendar - day 22](https://cdn.geotribu.fr/img/articles-blog-rdp/articles/2025/retrospective_qalendrier_2025/22_reprojection_lambert80zetrei.webp)

[_Lambert 93_](https://spatialreference.org/ref/epsg/2154/) is the widely used CRS in France, 93 is also the number of a department in the parisian suburbs where a lot of rappers grew up, and "80 zetrei" is 93 in [_verlan_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Verlan), upside down french slang.
